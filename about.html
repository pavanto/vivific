About Vivific

We are a consulting studio focused on clear thinking, strong architecture, and practical delivery support for modern software systems.

Vivific exists to help organisations move confidently through complex technology decisions. We have experience across public sector programmes, financial services platforms, and enterprise integration work.

Our approach is collaborative and calm. We work closely with delivery teams, internal architects, and vendors – simplifying where possible, highlighting trade-offs clearly, and documenting decisions in a way that future teams can follow.

How we like to work

  • Context first. We start with goals, constraints, and existing systems.
  • Simple where possible. We avoid complexity that teams cannot support.
  • Close to delivery. Architecture only helps when it’s connected to what is being built.
  • Transparent decisions. We document trade-offs and choices so they remain understandable later.
